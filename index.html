Our story

On our first day of college, we moved into Turman Hall at Emory University. We became quick friends and started dating later the same year. After graduating from Emory, Ariella worked in Connecticut as part of Teach For America while Josh worked with the Nationals in Washington, DC then started his PhD at the University of Michigan. In July 2017, Ariella joined Josh at the University of Michigan for medical school. In June 2018, Josh took Ariella on a trip to Traverse City, MI and proposed to her in a vineyard on Lake Michigan (they make wine in Michigan too!).
